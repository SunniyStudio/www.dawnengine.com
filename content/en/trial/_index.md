## About Licensing

DawnEngine is a **commercial engine** and is not available as a public free download. Start by **requesting a trial** for evaluation and technical validation, then choose the **commercial license** that fits your project for development and release.

## How to Get It

1. **Request a trial** — submit a trial request describing your team and project; we provide a trial package and license key.
2. **Validate** — evaluate the engine and domestic-OS adaptation during the trial period.
3. **Purchase a license** — choose Professional or Enterprise/Government based on team size and delivery needs.
4. **Ongoing support** — receive updates, patches, and technical support.

> Trial packages are distributed via a domestic CDN for fast access in China; trial keys and licensing details are provided by the sales team.

## Recommended System Requirements

- 64-bit multi-core CPU and a discrete GPU (Vulkan / DX12).
- 16GB+ RAM; 32GB+ recommended for large worlds and geospatial scenes.
- Ample disk space for assets and cache.
