## Product Roadmap

The plans below show DawnEngine's direction and priorities. Exact timing and scope may change with community feedback and release cadence.

## Near Term (Released / In Progress)

- **1.0 stable**: enhanced double-precision rendering, World Partition, HTN AI, Cesium geospatial (see [news](/en/news/)).
- **Localization**: stable support for Kylin and UOS; HarmonyOS in preview.

## Mid Term

- **Large-world collaboration**: improved partition-level multi-user collaboration and asset management.
- **Geographic twin enhancements**: richer 3D Tiles, photogrammetry, and real-time data ingestion.
- **Pixel streaming enhancements**: lower latency and easier-to-deploy cloud rendering.
- **Developer experience**: improved docs, sample projects, and toolchain.

## Long Term

- **Domestic GPU optimization**: performance and stability for domestic graphics hardware.
- **Expanded AI**: more decision and perception capabilities beyond [HTN](/en/features/htn-ai/).
- **Industry templates**: ready-to-use solutions for digital twins, simulation, and more.

> The roadmap is directional and not a delivery commitment. Join the discussion in the [community](/en/community/) to influence priorities.
