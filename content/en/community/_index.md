## Join the DawnEngine Community

DawnEngine grows with its community. Whether you are a developer, researcher, or industry user, you are welcome to take part.

## Channels

- **Developer forum**: ask questions, share experience, and help others.
- **Developer groups**: join the official groups for first-hand news.
- **Feedback & support**: report issues and propose features; licensed customers get priority support.

> Specific channel links will be provided here and in the footer at launch.

## Ways to Participate

- **Showcase**: share projects and industry cases built with DawnEngine.
- **Plugins & assets**: share community plugins, assets, and tools to grow the ecosystem (not engine source).
- **Docs & tutorials**: contribute know-how and tutorials to the [Learn](/en/learn/) section.
- **Feedback**: submit needs and suggestions to help shape the [roadmap](/en/roadmap/).

## Events

We host online talks, tech meetups, and developer events from time to time. Find announcements and recaps in the [news](/en/news/).

## Code of Conduct

Please be friendly, respectful, and professional in all interactions. We are committed to an open and inclusive community.
