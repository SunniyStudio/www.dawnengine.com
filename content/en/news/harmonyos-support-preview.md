Following Kylin and UOS, **DawnEngine's adaptation for HarmonyOS is now in preview**. This further completes DawnEngine's full-stack support for domestic operating systems.

## What's in the Preview

- Initial bring-up of the engine runtime and rendering pipeline on HarmonyOS.
- Ongoing adaptation and optimization for domestic GPUs and graphics stacks.
- Combined with [pixel streaming](/en/features/pixel-streaming/) for thin-client access.

## What's Next

We will continue to improve stability and performance in upcoming releases and provide more complete deployment documentation. Follow the [news](/en/news/) and [roadmap](/en/roadmap/) for progress.

Learn about the full [domestic OS support](/en/features/domestic-os/) capabilities.
