## An Open Yet Self-Controlled Ecosystem

DawnEngine is both a beneficiary of and contributor to the open-source ecosystem, and a member of the domestic hardware/software ecosystem. We build around four directions: **open foundation, domestic adaptation, industry solutions, and developer community.**

## Upstream & Open Source

- **O3DE community**: DawnEngine is built on [O3DE](/en/features/built-on-o3de/), tracking upstream releases and giving back to the community.
- **Cesium**: integrating [Cesium](/en/features/cesium-geospatial/) geospatial capabilities to carry 3D Tiles and real-Earth data.
- **Open standards**: embracing open formats such as glTF and 3D Tiles to lower data barriers.

## Localization Partners

DawnEngine works with domestic OS and chip vendors to complete [localization adaptation](/en/features/domestic-os/):

- **Kylin**
- **UOS**
- **HarmonyOS**

> Adaptation progress and certification info will be updated continuously in the [news](/en/news/).

## Industry Solutions

DawnEngine offers capability bundles and reference solutions for:

- **Digital twins**: city-, campus-, and watershed-scale twins and visualization.
- **Simulation**: situational simulation for transport, emergency response, and low-altitude scenarios.
- **Industrial visualization**: 3D presentation and interaction for equipment, energy, and manufacturing.
- **Geospatial**: reality meshes, photogrammetry, and GIS fusion.
