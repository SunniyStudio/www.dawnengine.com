## About DawnEngine

DawnEngine is a real-time 3D engine built on the open-source [O3DE](/en/features/built-on-o3de/), focused on **large-scale, high-precision, self-controlled** simulation and visualization.

## Our Mission

To let teams embrace the vitality of the open-source ecosystem while meeting real domestic needs for **self-control** and **high-precision large worlds** — from city-scale digital twins to planet-scale simulation.

## What We Believe

- **Open**: embrace open source and open standards; give back to the community.
- **Self-controlled**: invest in localization to ensure supply chain and compliance.
- **Pragmatic**: let real-world scenarios drive technical evolution.
